About Me

Hey! My name is Mihir and I'm a postgraduate student at UCL specialising in Data Science. My personal mission is to drive social impact through data.


During my undergraduate study of Economics at the University of Cambridge, my passion for data-driven analysis grew through Econometrics courses. Since then, I have gained industry experience completing a data science internship at Facebook where I worked on the Audience Network product. In November 2019, I led my team to win the best technical solution prize at the 2019 UCL Data Science Society Hackathon (in association with Microsoft and American Express).


In my free time, I love listening to all types of music, exploring new places and creating artwork (which I hope to post on this page at some point).
